## 1. Who we are

MyGamesList ("we", "us") provides a personal game library tracker. This document
explains, in placeholder form, what data we collect and how we use it.

## 2. Data we collect

- Account data you provide at sign-up (email, username).
- Your game library and the statuses you assign to games.
- Technical data needed to operate the service (for example, authentication
  tokens and basic device information).

## 3. How we use your data

- To create and secure your account.
- To store and display your game library.
- To operate, maintain, and improve the service.

## 4. Your rights

Subject to applicable law (including the LGPD), you may request access to,
correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Account export and deletion
are available from Settings.

## 5. Consent

When you create an account you accept this Privacy Policy and the Terms of
Service. We record the version of the documents you accepted.
